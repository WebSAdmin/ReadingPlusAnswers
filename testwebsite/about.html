Using the Search Engine

To get the best results out of your searches you should follow the below format when searching for story answers:

  • [story-name] optional:[level-of-story]
  • For example: GRAPEVINE, inc [Level I]

Not Getting your Desired Results?  Use the Advanced Search!

The Advanced search engine technique can help you do more narrow searches to yeild a greater chance of finding the answer you want

Follow the Below steps for the Advanced Searching Technique:

  1. [story-name] [level] [picture-of-title] :[The-current-year]
    • For example: Secrets of the Incas Level L Picture of a Mountain 2020

      • *Don't worry too much about remebering this format, it's not mandatory, this technique is best used if you can't find the answers to a story you're looking for.
